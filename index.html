    Welcome to our wedding site!

    Thank you for taking the time to visit our wedding site. Here you can RSVP, see venue location, accommodation options and hopefully find information on everything else you need to know about the day. If we’ve missed anything or you have any questions, please let us know.

    We look forward to seeing you all on the 30th June to celebrate!

    Ceremony

    Our wedding ceremony will take place at Zion United Church in Frampton Cotterell, starting at 12 noon. The ceremony will be followed by tea, coffee and nibbles at the church.

    Reception

    The ceremony will be followed by a wedding reception at Winterbourne Medieval Barn, which is a short drive from Zion Church. See our location page for more details.

    We will have a 3 course meal, followed by a ceilidh, and the reception will finish by 10pm. Please be aware that the barn is a medieval building with uneven floors and no heating, so wear suitable footwear and clothing. There is parking on site so please don’t park in the lane.
